VIRTUAL CLASSROOM

" The World is Our Classroom "

A virtual classroom is an online learning environment.  The environment can be web-based and accessed through a portal or software-based and require a downloadable and executable file.Just like in a real-world classroom, a student in a virtual classroom participates in synchronous instruction, which means that the teacher and students are logged into the virtual learning environment at the same time.
